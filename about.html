Our Story

It all started with a simple idea: create a space where everyone can feel like a superstar.

Welcome to the Party!

Charleston Pole Parties was born from a passion for dance and a desire to share the incredible confidence boost that comes from mastering something new and exciting. We believe that fitness should be fun, and that everyone deserves a space where they can let loose, laugh without reservation, and celebrate their bodies without judgment. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable experience that blends the athleticism of pole dance with the pure joy of a great party.

We saw a need for an alternative night out in Charleston—something more engaging than a typical bar scene, more empowering than a standard workout class. Our studio is that vision brought to life: a private, supportive, and high-energy environment dedicated entirely to you and your crew. We're not just teaching you moves; we're building a community one party at a time, creating lasting memories and helping you uncover the powerful, graceful star that's already inside you.
